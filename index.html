What is aug?

aug keeps track of the songs you listen to online, and creates a 'mosaic' dashboard of each release's album artwork. you can hover over each release to reveal more info about the songs you've listened to.

aug can recognize songs from just about any online music source. whatever the song, whatever the source, aug remembers for you... all in one place.

See what you hear.

aug's main dashboard is a simple mosaic of all the releases you've listened to online. hover over any release to see which tracks you heard from that release, when, how many times, at which site(s) and for how long.

Easily explore more songs, releases & artists

when you hear something you like, aug makes it easier than ever to explore the release and the artist. aug can automatically link you to the full album release for that song, at your choice of streaming sites or online music stores. when you visit those sites, aug can keep track of which songs you've already heard and which ones may be new to you, right in the external site.

Curate your auditions.

like to 'try before you buy?' aug can curate playlists at online stores to prioritize songs you haven't heard yet, or even remove the songs you've already heard. when you visit the most popular online DJ stores like Juno, Beatport, Bleep or Clone, aug can flag tracks you've already heard as visited links, so you can focus on the new stuff.

Break up with the songs you hate!

You've heard that song enough to last you a lifetime. Everybody else seems to love it (why?!), but guess what... you HATE it. Want to stop hearing it?

You can ghost songs, releases or even artists, which will automatically skip them in any online playlist, no matter the site or app. Your exes will still display, so you can easily unghost them with one click. Or you can unghost once, to check if they've changed their ways before you get back together.

aug isn't just for online music stores... it's for the whole web.

aug works on free streaming sites too. soundcloud, youtube, spotify free or premium... all are compatible with aug. if the song can be identified by popular song recognition apps like shazam or soundhound, it will be recognized by aug.

both the app and the browser plug-in recognize everything you listen to online, regardless of whether you listen in a proprietary app or a different browser.

Your audio, your privacy.

aug never sells your individual data or shares it with any external companies. we do share aggregate data with others, including you, about the most popular songs among all of aug's users on our Charts.
